## About The Project

![Product Name Screen Shot][product-screenshot]

Film Roulette is an app designed to help you easily find a great movie to watch. With access to a database of 1000 top-rated movies, this app allows you to quickly spin the "Film Roulette" wheel and let chance determine your viewing experience. Whether you're in the mood for a classic, a drama, a comedy, or something in between, Film Roulette ensures that you'll never be stuck searching for a movie again. So sit back, relax, and let Film Roulette help you find your next cinematic adventure.
